About Invitx

Invitx, is a leading online invitation creation platform that simplifies the process of creating and sharing personalized webpages for events. We offer a user-friendly interface, customizable designs, and seamless integration with various features.

Key Features:

  • User-friendly, attractive, and customized online invitations.
  • Customers can easily share their invitation webpage/card with friends and family.
  • Invitations are designed to be mobile-friendly and responsive.
  • Integration with Google Maps, users can obtain directions to the event venue.
  • Save the Date Feature, allowing recipients to easily save the event date and time to their calendars.
  • Countdown Timer, to build excitement and remind guests about the upcoming event.
  • Offers customizable design options, to match their event theme and personal preferences.
  • Secure and Reliable, protecting user information and preventing unauthorized access.
